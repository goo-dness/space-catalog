Messier Objects

In 1771, Charles Messier catalogued 110 celestial objects to avoid confusing them with comets. What he dismissed as distractions became some of the most studied objects in the universe — galaxies, nebulae, and star clusters that African astronomers had observed for centuries before his catalogue existed
